Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 19, 2025

At Vspot, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience with our application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Vspot.

1. Information We Collect

Vspot is designed with privacy in mind. The application primarily stores data locally on your device:

  • Clipboard Content: Vspot captures and stores clipboard content (text, code, images, etc.) that you copy while using your Mac. This data is stored locally on your device only.
  • Usage Patterns: Basic app usage statistics such as feature usage frequency and performance metrics (stored locally).
  • Application Preferences: Your settings, customizations, and organizational preferences within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve the Vspot experience:

  • Organizing and categorizing your clipboard history
  • Providing search functionality across your stored content
  • Maintaining your custom tabs and organizational structure
  • Improving app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: All your clipboard data, notes, and app preferences are stored locally on your Mac. Vspot does not automatically sync or transmit your personal content to external servers or cloud services.

Security Measures: Your data is protected by:

  • macOS built-in security features and encryption
  • Secure local storage using Apple's recommended practices
  • No unauthorized network transmission of personal content

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Vspot does not share, sell, or transmit your personal clipboard content to third parties. Your data remains on your device under your control.

No Third-Party Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics services that would have access to your personal content.

5. User Control and Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Clear Data: Use the "Clear All" function to delete clipboard history at any time
  • Selective Deletion: Delete individual items or categories of content as needed
  • App Uninstallation: Uninstalling Vspot will remove all associated data from your device
  • Export Control: You choose what data to export and when

6. macOS Permissions

Vspot requires certain macOS permissions to function:

  • Accessibility Access: To monitor clipboard changes and provide seamless capture
  • File System Access: To save and organize your content locally

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and are managed through macOS security settings.

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • The updated date at the top of this policy will be changed
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the app or our website
  • Continued use of Vspot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

8. Children's Privacy

Vspot is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. International Users

Vspot is designed for global use. Since all data is stored locally on your device, international data transfer concerns are minimized. However, users should be aware of their local privacy laws and regulations.

10. California Privacy Rights

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since Vspot stores data locally and does not sell personal information, most CCPA provisions do not apply. However, you always have the right to delete your data by using the app's built-in deletion features.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how Vspot handles your data, please contact us:

Email: privacy@vspot.app
Support: support@vspot.app

We will respond to privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.
