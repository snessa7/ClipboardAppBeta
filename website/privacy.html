Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

Vspot! is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data and ensure your information remains secure.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Vspot! operates entirely on your local machine without any external data transmission.

What Vspot! Accesses:

  • Clipboard Content: Vspot! monitors your system clipboard to build your clipboard history. This data never leaves your device.
  • Local Storage: All clipboard items, notes, and custom tabs are stored locally on your Mac in encrypted format.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Clipboard history is stored in your app's local container
  • Sensitive items can be encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • No cloud synchronization or external servers are used
  • Data is automatically cleaned based on your retention settings

Data Sharing

Vspot! does not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. Your clipboard data, notes, and custom tabs remain entirely on your device.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • App Sandboxing to isolate the app from other system resources
  • Optional AES-256 encryption for sensitive clipboard items
  • Secure data deletion when items are removed
  • No network permissions prevent any accidental data transmission

User Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all stored data directly within the app
  • Deletion: Delete individual items or clear all data instantly
  • Export: Export your notes and custom tabs for backup
  • Control: Configure retention periods and automatic cleanup

Permissions

Vspot! requests minimal permissions:

  • Clipboard Access: Required to monitor and manage clipboard history
  • File System (Sandboxed): To save your data locally within the app container

Analytics and Tracking

Vspot! does not include any analytics, tracking, or telemetry. We do not collect usage statistics, crash reports, or any other data about how you use the app.

Children's Privacy

Vspot! does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for general productivity use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of Vspot! after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or Vspot!'s privacy practices, please contact us at:

support@vspot.app

Compliance

Vspot! complies with:

  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
  • macOS App Sandbox requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) requirements
